About Sand Canvas

Hi, I'm Ayush — an indie app/web developer and game dev enthusiast with a love for pixel art and creative coding. Sand Canvas is a playful sandbox simulator I built to let you create mesmerizing sand art right in your browser. It's a fun project made with heart, letting you draw, blend, and express freely with falling grains of digital sand. Try it out, share your creations, and most of all — have fun!

If you like what you see or want to connect, feel free to check out my portfolio!

If you enjoyed using Sand Canvas or just want to support my creative experiments, feel free to buymeacoffee — it helps keep the pixels flowing ☕✨
